About

Relationships can improve your social life, giving you more influence and opportunities. You can make acquaintances, friends, close friends, and gain family members by boosting your relationships and increasing interactions with people of a lower tier. Being friends with important people can increase your prestige and benefit your career.

Friend Score

A friend score is given to each person you meet, which determines how close you are to them. The higher the score, the closer the relationship. You can increase your friend score by interacting with people, such as talking to them, helping them, or spending time with them. Your friend score will directly affect the relationship tier. You will also have a friend score with other people.

Family Score

A family score is given to each family member, which determines how close you are to them. The higher the score, the closer the relationship. Your family score can be increased through regular contact, meaningful conversations, emotional support, and quality time. Your friend score will directly affect the relationship tier. You will also have a family score with other family members.
